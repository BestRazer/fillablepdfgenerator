Digital Worksheet generator BETA

I've always hated manually filling out scanned worksheets by dragging and dropping text fields. So, I created this website to automatically create a fillable pdf out of a picture of a worksheet.

To get started, select a file. The minimum length slider sets the minimum length a line has to be to become a fillable field, the standard value should usually be fine. Once you've uploaded your image, press 'Upload & Fill' and your PDF should automatically be downloaded!
